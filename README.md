## 🧭 About Me

I'm a Generative AI / ML Engineer focused on taking LLM systems from prototype to production. Currently **Engineer II at GEICO**, building Gen-AI digital experiences on event-driven microservices. Previously led an 8-engineer team at **Flatirons AI** delivering Graph-RAG and RAPTOR retrieval pipelines for Fintech.

I hold an **M.S. in Artificial Intelligence** from San Jose State University and care about systems that are **reliable, scalable, and observable** — not just demos that work once.

> Build AI systems that are reliable, scalable, and production-ready.

## 🚀 What I'm Building

- 🤖 **Agentic AI** — multi-agent orchestration with LangGraph, tool calling, and evaluation pipelines
- 🕸️ **GraphRAG** — knowledge-graph retrieval (Neo4j, RAPTOR, hybrid search) for complex enterprise reasoning
- 🧠 **LLM Applications** — fine-tuning, RAG, and structured generation across OpenAI / Llama / Gemini / Mistral
- ⚙️ **AI Infrastructure** — event-driven microservices, Kubernetes, and full-stack observability
- 📈 **Quantitative Intelligence** — an AI-native trading platform with research, strategy, and backtesting agents
